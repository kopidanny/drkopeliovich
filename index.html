About

Meet
Dr. Dan Kopeliovich

Dr. Dan Kopeliovich is an Oral & Maxillofacial Surgeon with dual-degree training and international surgical expertise. He holds both a medical degree (MD) from Tel Aviv University and a dental degree (DMD) from Hebrew University Jerusalem, providing the comprehensive perspective that complex implant cases require.

He completed his oral and maxillofacial surgery residency in Israel, followed by advanced fellowship training at the University of Maryland in head and neck surgery and microvascular reconstruction — disciplines that develop the anatomical knowledge and surgical precision essential for advanced implant procedures.

Dr. Kopeliovich's clinical focus is on patients who need more than routine implant placement: those requiring full mouth reconstruction, complex bone grafting, or revision of previous implant work.

Treatment Focus

Exclusively
Advanced Procedures

Dr. Kopeliovich's practice is focused exclusively on advanced implant procedures and complex reconstruction — not routine implant placement.

01

Full Mouth Dental Implants

Complete arch replacement for patients who have lost most or all of their teeth. Strategic implant placement and comprehensive prosthetic planning to restore full dental function.

02

All-on-4 / All-on-6

Fixed prosthetic solutions using four to six implants to support a complete arch. These protocols can often be completed with reduced surgical time and immediate provisional teeth.

03

Complex Implant Reconstruction

Surgical treatment for challenging cases including failed previous implants, severe bone loss, or anatomical complications requiring specialized surgical expertise.

04

Bone Grafting

Procedures to rebuild jawbone structure lost due to tooth extraction, disease, or trauma. Adequate bone volume is essential for successful long-term implant outcomes.

05

Second Opinions

Independent evaluation of existing treatment plans or assessment of cases deemed too complex. A fresh surgical perspective can clarify options and establish realistic expectations.

Surgical Expertise

Why Choose an Oral &
Maxillofacial Surgeon

🦴

Surgical Training in Bone & Anatomy

OMS surgeons complete years of hospital-based surgical residency focused specifically on the bones, nerves, and tissues of the face and jaws — the exact structures navigated during implant surgery.

⚙️

Managing Complex Cases

Advanced cases often involve insufficient bone, proximity to critical nerves or sinuses, or multiple coordinated surgical procedures. These require planning and execution that draws on specialized training.

🛡️

Handling Complications

Surgical complications, while uncommon, do occur. A surgeon trained for complex anatomical situations is better prepared to address unexpected findings and minimize risk.

🔭

Reconstruction-Oriented Thinking

Rather than simply placing implants, a reconstructive approach considers long-term function and stability of the entire jaw — especially important for patients with bone loss or previous failures.

"For straightforward implant cases, many qualified practitioners can provide excellent care. For complex cases, the additional surgical training of an oral and maxillofacial surgeon can make a meaningful difference in outcomes."

If you have been told your case is complex, involves significant bone loss, or have had previous implant failures, a consultation with a surgical specialist is strongly recommended before proceeding.

How It Works

Treatment Process for
International Patients

A clear, practical process designed for patients traveling from the US and Canada.

1

Online Evaluation

Submit your case information and any existing records for review. Dr. Kopeliovich will assess your situation and discuss candidacy, options, and estimated costs.

2

Travel to Cancún

Once a treatment plan is confirmed, coordinate your travel dates. Most patients arrange a stay of 3–10 days. We can provide guidance on accommodations and logistics.

3

Procedure & Treatment

Your surgical procedure is performed at Mark Shtern Dental Clinic, a state-of-the-art private facility in Cancún. Dr. Kopeliovich will walk you through each step and post-operative care before you leave.

4

Healing & Follow-Up

Most patients return home within days. Dr. Kopeliovich remains available for remote follow-up. Some cases may require a second visit for final prosthetic work.
